About Me

A software developer with a passion and skillset for data processing and automation. Throughout my career I've seen unformed data/metadata and unautomated processes sapping scientists' time and their confidence in results. At each turn, I've had the opportunity to improve the status quo, learn more about the science involved (which satisfies my inner physics major), and build out my skills around deployment and DevOps. It is my goal anywhere I go not just to create and improve the systems they need but also to teach and learn as much as possible about the flow of information through the scientific process.

My life away from the computer consists mostly of playing or training for frisbee, reading, and playing piano.

About This Site

Built with Flask, stylized with Tailwind CSS, and deployed "serverlessly" on AWS with Zappa.

This site is an ongoing experiment in web design and deployment for me. It began as a PHP-based site using Laravel and running on an EC2 instance (costing me ~$10-20/month). From there it progressed to being a collection of statically sourced HTML pages from an S3 bucket (for free!) and then to it's current dynamic incarnation (also for free!). There are absolutely no fun easter-eggs so don't bother looking.
